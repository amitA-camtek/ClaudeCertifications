# W12 — Final Exam Prep

**Domain:** Exam Prep · Review & Practice (All Domains)
**Budget:** 2 days × 2.5 h = 5 h
**Goal:** walk into exam day with ≥ 720/1000 readiness.

## Study Day (2.5 h)

| Time | Block | Task |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:10 | Warmup | Pull 2 weakest domains from W11 `notes/weak_spots.md` |
| 0:10–1:00 | Targeted review | Weakest domain — re-read task statements, re-do missed Q's |
| 1:00–1:50 | Targeted review | 2nd-weakest domain — same drill |
| 1:50–2:30 | Exam | **Full Practice Exam 2** (50 Q) — start; finish in buffer if needed |

## Practice Day (2.5 h)

| Time | Block | Task |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:10 | Warmup | Review Exam 2 results |
| 0:10–1:40 | Exam | **Full Practice Exam 3** (50 Q, **timed**) |
| 1:40–2:15 | Review | Wrong answers, focus on anti-patterns and gotchas |
| 2:15–2:30 | Final | Light review of key concepts — `notes/exam_day_cheatsheet.md` |

**Exam day follows immediately after this week.**

**Deliverables:** Practice Exams 2 & 3 reviews in `practice_test/`, final cheatsheet in `notes/exam_day_cheatsheet.md`.
